We are DataThings

We are a startup of four passionate researchers holding PhDs in Computer Science. After several years of successful collaboration at the University of Luxembourg/SnT, we founded DataThings to start the next chapter in live data analytics. We aim at improving live analytics over complex and time-evolving data in order to support your businesses in operational decision-making. Please visit our website to find out more
