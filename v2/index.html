about the mission

"Making sustainable operational decisions is sometimes hard. At DataThings we believe that the large amount of data collected today has the potential to help better understanding businesses, environment, physical phenomenaâ€™s, bodies, health, and nearly every other aspect of our daily lives. DataThings mission is to transform your data into actionable and valuable insights in order to support you in making the best operational decisions. Created as a spin-off of a research University, DataThings leverages novel live analytics and machine learning algorithms. Instead of only aggregating data to explain the past, this allows to anticipate the future and to simulate the impacts of different decisions.
